World Migration Project

Welcome to our interactive world migration data visualization website.
Through charts and maps, we hope to help you understand more about global migration patterns and their relationship with migrants' life expectancy and GDP. The visualizations show the immigration and emigration data, migration flows between countries, and the impact on migrants' quality of life. They suggest the correlation between migration and life expectancy, as well as the economic through GDP analysis. Customize your exploration by filtering countries, and time periods to gain valuable insights into the complex dynamics of international migration.
